Here are some highly rated employee monitoring tools used throughout the world!

Atera Homepage

Atera is an employee device monitoring software. Atera products are priced via a pay pertechnician price. This means that you do not need to pay more as you grow or add devices, but instead, when you require more technicians. Atera also offers 24/7 support, a variety of service plans, multiple management systems, and has a very friendly user interface.

Hikvision Homepage

Hikvision is one of the biggest CCTV(Closed-Circuit Television) Camera Brands of 2021. They provide multiple camera styles as well as mounting systems for said cameras. They also provide software that involves Artificial Intelligence. They also provide multiple other products such as access control and video intercom systems. Besides working in the security industry they have exteded their reach to smart home tech, industrial automation, and automotive electronic industries to acheive a long-term vision.

Axis Communications Homepage

Axis is another one of the biggest CCTV(Closed-Circuit Television) Camera Brands of 2021. Axis Communications is a Swedish based manufacturer. They provide products such as network cameras, access control, and audio devices for both physical and video surveillance industries. Axis is the market leader in network video, and they are driving the industry by continually launching products based on an open platform. This allows them to deliver high value images to their customers.
