About

Employee monitoring is as it states in the name the activity of businesses monitoring employees. there are a variety of reasons for why a business would want to monitor its employees. A few examples would be to increase efficiency in the workflow of employees, to detect and prevent data breaches, and to track the amount of work an employee has done. Business will use a variety of tools to monitor its employees. A few of the tools business use to monitor employees are programs to monitor what applications employees are running on there computers, Cameras around the workplace and webcams for each employee, and GPS tracking using their mobile device. These tools are used to gather data that will be used to improve the business in the ways that are mentions above.
